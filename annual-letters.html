Annual Letters

Each year around June 6 — the day TradingTransformer Technologies LLC was incorporated — CEO Liwei Wu shares a letter on where we are, what we’ve learned, and where we’re headed.

  • 2026 · June 6
    NoArk sails out, and is vibe trading the next wave after vibe coding?

    The year NoArk sailed out on the App Store — our first product in your hands, and the milestone Liwei is proudest of — alongside a 90% return in the Shanghai market, our third after the U.S. and Hong Kong. He reflects on whether vibe trading follows vibe coding, why agentic trading seems to thrive in China, our fiat lux pledge to put half of future profits into education, and Claude Shannon’s prediction that AI returns could one day track Moore’s Law.

  • 2025 · June 6
    A Letter from our CEO

    The first letter with real evidence that the method works: a 76% return in the U.S. and 81% in Hong Kong, far ahead of the S&P 500 and Hang Seng over the same period. Liwei traces how a PhD “final-year project” quietly became a six-year journey into AI investing, remembers the role models who shaped it, and shares the hard-won lessons he carries forward.
