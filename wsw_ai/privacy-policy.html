Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 20, 2026

Introduction

NoArk ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the app and the information we don't collect from you when you use our application. NoArk does not require you to create an account or sign in.

Information We Don't Collect

NoArk is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store on our servers, or transmit personal information about you to us, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number)
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Location information
  • Chat history or app activity on our servers

Data Stored on Your Device

NoArk may save information locally on your device only—such as AI Persona chat history, preferences, and other app data—to provide the app experience. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

NoArk displays news content from publicly available RSS feeds from Bloomberg, CNBC, and other publishers. When you choose to read a full article, you will be directed to the respective website using Safari View Controller within the app. Please refer to each publisher's privacy policy for information about how they may collect and use your data when you visit their websites.

AI Features

NoArk includes AI-powered features, including news analysis and AI Persona chats. When you use these features, your messages and the content you choose to analyze may be sent to our AI service providers to generate responses. We do not retain or store your queries or chat content on our servers after a request is complete. Any chat history or related data shown in the app is stored only on your device locally.

Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
