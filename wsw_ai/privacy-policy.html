Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 29, 2025

Introduction

WSW AI ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the app and the information we don't collect from you when you use our application.

Information We Don't Collect

WSW AI is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number)
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Location information
  • Search history within the app

Authentication

Our app uses a simple key-based authentication system. The authentication keys are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

WSW AI displays news content from publicly available RSS feeds from Bloomberg and CNBC. When you choose to read a full article, you will be directed to the respective website using Safari View Controller within the app. Please refer to Bloomberg's and CNBC's privacy policies for information about how they may collect and use your data when you visit their websites.

AI Features

Our app includes AI-powered analysis features. When you use these features, your queries and the news content you choose to analyze are processed by our AI service providers. We use these services in a way that does not retain or store your queries after the analysis is complete.

Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
